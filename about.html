About Deccanevents

Empowering runners, building communities, and creating positive change through world-class marathon events.

Our Mission

At FitForce Club, we believe in the power of running to transform lives, build communities, and create positive change. Since our founding, we've been dedicated to organizing world-class marathon events that challenge participants while supporting meaningful causes.

We strive to create inclusive, professionally managed events that celebrate the spirit of running while making a tangible difference in the communities we serve. Every race we organize is designed to inspire personal achievement and collective impact.

Our Story

Founded by passionate runners who wanted to create more than just races, FitForce Club has grown into Maharashtra's premier marathon event organization. We've helped thousands of runners achieve their personal bests while raising funds for important social causes.

What started as a small group of running enthusiasts has evolved into a professional organization that hosts multiple events throughout the year, each designed to challenge participants while contributing to community development initiatives.

Key Milestones

  • • 10+ successful marathon events organized
  • • 5,000+ runners participated across all events
  • • ₹50+ lakhs raised for various charitable causes
  • • 100+ community partnerships established
