Event Overview

The Sinhagad Marathon offers runners an unforgettable experience through one of Maharashtra's most historic and scenic locations. Join us for a challenging run that combines fitness, heritage, and community spirit.

Why Sinhagad Marathon?

Scenic Route

Run through breathtaking landscapes and historic fort views

Community Spirit

Join fellow runners in a celebration of fitness and heritage

Professional Organization

World-class event management and safety measures

Charity Support
